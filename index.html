How It’s Made

A SCOBY (Symbiotic Consortium of Bacteria and Yeast) ferments sweetened tea into a naturally effervescent drink called kombucha — rich in probiotics, organic acids, and refreshing tangy flavors.

Each small batch of Cosmos Kombucha is handcrafted through traditional fermentation, ensuring every sip is alive with nature’s microbial magic.

How to Drink

Enjoy Cosmos Kombucha any time of day.

Chilled — straight from the bottle With meals — aids digestion Post-workout — rehydrates In smoothies/mocktails — fizzy twist Post-dinner — overnight renewal

Instructions

  • Chill before serving — best enjoyed cold.
  • Do not shake; gently swirl if sediment forms (it’s natural).
  • Consume within 5 days of opening for best taste and probiotics.
  • Not advised for children under 5 years.
  • Pregnant and breastfeeding women should consult a doctor before consuming.
  • Serving size: 70–100 ml per adult, 40–50 ml for children.
